The key pattern · timing

People cook before the holiday, not on it

Good Friday ran +24% above trend (Easter prep), and Ascension / Vatertag +12% (BBQ season). But the holy days themselves dip — Easter Sunday -19%, Easter Monday -9% — the audience is offline at the table. Publish & refresh holiday recipes 5-7 days early; the demand peak is the planning day.
